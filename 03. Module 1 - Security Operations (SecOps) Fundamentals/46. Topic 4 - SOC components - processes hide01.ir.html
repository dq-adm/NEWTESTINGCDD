SOC components - Processes

 

Figure - zoom in


We have seen that security has three core pillars: people, technology, and processes. We have explained the first two, and it's time to discuss the third one; processes. A process is a series of steps and decisions involved in the way work is completed. You may not realize it, but processes are everywhere around us. The way you prepare your morning coffee, order from Amazon, and drive your car are examples of processes.

Security is no different and involves a set of processes and supporting documents to operate and organize the work. In the following lessons, we will discuss popular documents used in security operations and Cyberdefense.
