Phase 3: Containment, Eradication, and Recovery



So far, we have spent some time preparing and were able to detect the attack. The remaining part of the IR exercise can be summarized into:

  • Stop further damage => Containment.
  • Fix present damage and prevent it from happening again => Eradication.
  • Restore clean-state => Recovery.

Let's discuss each one of the three phases in detail!
