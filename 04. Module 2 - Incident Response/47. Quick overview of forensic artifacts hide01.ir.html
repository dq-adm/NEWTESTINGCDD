A quick overview of forensic artifacts

In DFIR, you will always hear the word artifacts. Like your hand's fingerprints or your DNA, the digital equivalent for that is the forensic artifacts. 

Artifacts can be in many forms: registry keys, Windows processes, files, timestamps, IP addresses, etc. 

This lab will show how we can utilize Velociraptor to collect some of the artifacts mentioned above, and later on, in the DFIR module, we will discuss this in depth.
