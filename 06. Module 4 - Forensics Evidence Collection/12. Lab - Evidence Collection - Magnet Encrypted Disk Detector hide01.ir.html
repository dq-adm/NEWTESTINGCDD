2-Check Encrypted drives using Magnet Encrypted Disk Detector.

Checking for disk encryption is important before acquiring a triage and disk images. The tool you will use is EDD. It scans local drives for encryption signatures such as TrueCrypt, VeraCrypt, and Bitlocker. To list available tool options, execute "EDDv310.exe /?" in cmd.
